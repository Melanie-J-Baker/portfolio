About me

I am a highly motivated junior Software Engineer. I taught myself the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, NodeJS, Express, and MongoDB alongside running a gardening business, and soon realised I was in the wrong career. Since then I have been developing my skills (I have completed the Odin Project Foundations and Full Stack JavaScript courses, amongst others), and am now looking for employment within the sector. I have always been independent and passionate about learning, making the most of web resources to teach myself new things (including mechanics, plumbing, and even hula hoop tricks). Coding enables me to find solutions to real-world problems, while at the same time being creative. I am looking forward to honing my skills as my career in development progresses.
